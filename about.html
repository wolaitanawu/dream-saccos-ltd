About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and commitment to your financial success

Our Story

Dream Saccos LTD was founded in 2026 as a community-focused financial cooperative aimed at empowering members in South Ethiopia. We are a newly registered Saving and Credit Cooperative officially registered by the Ethiopian Government.

Our mission is to provide accessible and affordable financial services to our community. We believe in the power of collective savings and cooperative principles to help our members achieve their financial goals and improve their quality of life.

As a newly established SACCO, we are committed to building strong foundations based on integrity, transparency, and member empowerment. We invite you to join us on this journey towards financial freedom and community development.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and sustainable financial services that empower our members to achieve their economic goals and improve their quality of life. We are committed to promoting a culture of savings, providing credit facilities, and fostering financial literacy within our community.

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted savings and credit cooperative in Ethiopia, recognized for our innovative financial solutions, member-centric approach, and significant contribution to the economic empowerment of our community. We envision a future where every member achieves financial freedom.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our operations and interactions. Honesty, transparency, and accountability are the foundations of our relationship with members.

Transparency

We maintain open and honest communication with all our stakeholders. Every transaction, decision, and policy is communicated clearly to build trust and confidence.

Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions and deliver on our promises. Our members can count on us to honor our commitments and act in their best interests.

Member Empowerment

We are dedicated to empowering our members to achieve financial independence. Through education, access to credit, and supportive services, we help members build a secure future.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to provide better financial solutions for our members. From mobile banking to new loan products, we embrace technology and creative solutions.

Community

We believe in the power of community and collective action. Together, we achieve more than any individual could alone. Our cooperative structure reflects this core belief.
